# Bounded logo similarity screen

Date: 2026-07-18

This is a preliminary collision screen for the TLAC owl mark. It is not a
comprehensive clearance search or legal opinion.

## Search boundary

Reviewed public web and image results for:

- `half lidded owl head logo software company`
- `sleepy skeptical owl logo brand`
- `owl face logo AI technology company`
- `"The Little AI Company" owl logo`
- `owl logo AI company software half lidded`

Also checked the public USPTO search index for the exact company name and
reviewed the USPTO guidance for owl design-code searching.

## Closest results reviewed

- Sleepy Owl Coffee: a minimal purple/black owl used for coffee products.
- Owl.co: a word-led insurance-technology identity.
- Owl Labs: a camera and meeting-hardware identity.
- Samsara: a minimal geometric operations-software mark.
- Vigilant AI's Canadian owl design registration: a red-and-gold stylized owl
  used for records and data-management software.

## Assessment

No result reviewed is conspicuously similar to the TLAC mark's combined
features: detailed rumpled feather silhouette, asymmetric half-lidded eyes,
espresso/cream/rust/teal palette, and editorial hand-drawn treatment.

The most relevant caution is that relaxed owl eyes are already used by Sleepy
Owl Coffee. TLAC is differentiated by its name, detailed silhouette, palette,
full mascot system, and practical-AI field. Preserve those distinctions and do
not simplify the mark toward their minimal circular purple owl.
