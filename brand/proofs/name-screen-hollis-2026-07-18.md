# Hollis name screen — 2026-07-18

This is a bounded creative collision screen, not legal clearance.

## Screen performed

- Exact web searches for `"Hollis the owl"`, `"Hollis" owl mascot`, and
  `"Hollis shipped it"` found no obvious prominent owl character, software
  mascot, or release campaign using the same complete identity.
- The official USPTO search entry point and likelihood-of-confusion guidance
  were reviewed:
  - <https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/search/search>
  - <https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/search/likelihood-confusion>

## Limits

- `Hollis` is an existing personal name and surname.
- Exact-phrase web results do not cover unregistered common-law use, state
  registrations, international registrations, confusingly similar names, or
  related goods and services.
- The USPTO warns that marks can conflict through similar sound, appearance,
  meaning, or commercial impression even when they are not identical.

## Decision

Use `Hollis, the Little AI Company owl` as the approved character identity for
company storytelling and release announcements. Do not describe the name as
registered, cleared, exclusive, or legally protected until a professional
clearance process supports that claim.
